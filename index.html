Synth sETH

Synth sETH is an experimental blockchain asset created solely for academic and research purposes in decentralized finance (DeFi) environments. It is not intended for public investment or financial speculation.

The token is designed to simulate synthetic Ether behavior for interoperability testing, liquidity experiments, and cross-chain analytics within controlled, non-production scenarios.

This project aims to study decentralized finance mechanisms without any financial risk or obligation.
